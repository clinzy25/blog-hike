Devil's Dome Loop

August 2025 | 4 days | 40 miles | +10200' | 

Caltopo Link

This was my first substantial trip of the 2025 season, I am writing a couple of months after so this will be more of a photo dump. Trip report here.

The first day was 10 flat miles through the forest along Ross Lake. This part can be skipped by water taxi.

Day 2 consisted entirely of a 5000' climb up to Devil's Dome.

Blueberries were plentiful and delicious.

Views from the dome on arrival.

This trail circumnavigates Jack Mountain, a 9000' Bulger peak.

Sunset was a treat.

Clouds rolled in in the morning.

Some top tier ridge hiking on Day 3.

Continuing around the loop.

The last day was a swift 10 mile descent out to east bank TH. The attached map includes a Crater Mountain ascent that I passed on.
